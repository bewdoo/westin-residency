Last updated: 22 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how WELLSOLVE CORP PRIVATE LIMITED, operating as Realty Unplugged ("we", "us", "our"), collects, uses, shares and protects your personal information when you visit our website or submit an enquiry. By using this website, you consent to the practices described below.

Information you provide: when you submit the enquiry form, we collect your name, phone number, email address, and your residence preference.

Information collected automatically: your IP address, browser and device type, pages viewed, referring website, and similar usage data via cookies and tracking technologies.

Marketing & attribution identifiers: when you arrive from an advertisement, we may capture click identifiers such as gclid (Google) and fbclid (Meta), along with UTM campaign parameters, to understand which campaign referred you.

We use cookies and similar technologies, including the Meta Pixel, Google Ads and Google tag, to measure conversions, attribute leads to advertising campaigns, and show relevant ads. These third parties may set their own cookies. You can manage cookies through your browser settings and opt out of personalised ads via Google Ad Settings and Meta Ad Preferences.

We may share your information with:

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry and for legitimate business or legal purposes, after which it is securely deleted or anonymised.
