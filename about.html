About Us

Who We Are

WELLSOLVE CORP PRIVATE LIMITED, operating as Realty Unplugged, is an authorized marketing partner for Whiteland Corporation. We help discerning home-buyers and investors discover, understand, and acquire premium residences across Gurugram with clarity and confidence.

Our role is advisory and facilitative: we present verified project information, arrange private site visits, and guide you through every step of the buying journey — while the development itself is built and delivered by the promoter, Whiteland Corporation Pvt. Ltd.

The Project

We are proud to represent The Westin Residences at Whiteland's Urban Resort, Sector 103, Dwarka Expressway, Gurugram — India's first Westin-operated residences. Conceived in partnership with Marriott International, it brings the timeless elegance of a five-star hotel to private residential living, set across 22 acres with 30+ world-class amenities.

What We Do

  • Personalised property advisory for 3 & 4 BHK residences and penthouses
  • Private, scheduled site and experience-centre visits
  • Transparent guidance on pricing, inventory, RERA status and documentation
  • End-to-end support from first enquiry through to booking

Our Commitment

We believe luxury begins with trust. Every detail we share is drawn from official developer disclosures, and we encourage every prospective purchaser to independently verify all project details and RERA registrations before making a decision.
